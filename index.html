Who I Am

Still early in
the journey.

My name is Jahidul Hassan Parvez. I'm a student, which basically means I spend a lot of time learning things I didn't know I needed to learn. That part I've made peace with.

Photography started because I kept seeing things I didn't want to forget. A weird shadow on a wall. A moment that lasted about two seconds. Turns out your phone is actually really good at capturing those — you just have to remember to look.

Music is something I can't really explain well enough for it to make sense in text. And mountains — standing somewhere high and quiet does something to how you think about everything else. I recommend it.
